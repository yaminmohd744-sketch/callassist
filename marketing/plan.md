## 3. Posting Schedule

### Week structure (repeat every week)

| Day | Platform | Format | Angle |
|-----|----------|--------|-------|
| Monday | Instagram | Carousel | Educational (objection scripts / framework) |
| Tuesday | TikTok | AI UGC Video | Pain/validation |
| Wednesday | Instagram | Reel (repurpose video) | Product POV |
| Thursday | TikTok | AI UGC Video | Price comparison or secret weapon |
| Friday | Instagram | Carousel | Competitor comparison or product walkthrough |
| Saturday | TikTok | Screen recording | POV close probability / demo |
| Sunday | Instagram | Single image quote | Punchy one-liner (shareable) |

**TikTok bonus:** Post at 7am, 12pm, and 6pm when possible — TikTok rewards volume.

### Month 1 — Warmup (build content library, test hooks)
- Produce all 10 video scripts above using HeyGen or Arcads
- Produce all 5 carousels in Canva Pro / Figma
- Post Monday–Friday only, 1x per platform per day
- Goal: find which hook type gets the most profile visits

### Month 2 — Scale up
- Double posting frequency on whichever platform performed better in Month 1
- Introduce comment-trigger CTAs ("Comment PITCH and I'll DM you the script")
- A/B test waitlist CTA vs download CTA on same content

### Month 3 — Optimise
- Kill bottom 20% of content formats
- Double down on top 2 angles by engagement and link-in-bio conversions
- Introduce repost/collab with sales influencers (1–3 DMs per week)

## 4. CTA Split Strategy

### Rule: Match CTA to where the viewer is

| Viewer situation | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|-----------------|-------------|---------------|
| On Windows, ready to act | "Get for Windows — free trial" | — |
| On mobile / iPhone | "Join the waitlist" | — |
| Skeptical (first touch) | "Join the waitlist — no card" | "See how it works →" |
| Already warm (retarget / second view) | "Get for Windows" | "7-day money-back" |
| Sales Manager | "Get for Windows — team plans" | "Join the waitlist" |

### In-video CTA delivery (word-for-word)

**Download CTA (Electron users):**
> "Free trial at pitchr.org — link in bio. Windows 10 and 11. Takes 60 seconds."

**Waitlist CTA (web / mobile):**
> "Join 1,800+ reps on the waitlist at pitchr.org — link in bio. No card required."

**Split CTA (end of carousel slide):**
> "On Windows? Download free. Not yet? Join the waitlist. Either way — pitchr.org"

### Link in bio setup

Use a simple link page (Linktree or single landing page at pitchr.org):
1. **Get for Windows — Free Trial** → Desktop download CTA
2. **Join the Waitlist** → pitchr.org/waitlist (the waitlist screen you just built)
3. **See it in action** → Demo video or landing page features section
