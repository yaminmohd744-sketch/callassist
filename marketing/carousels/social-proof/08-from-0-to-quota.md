## TikTok text overlay / voiceover notes
**Voiceover tone:** Storytelling mode — like recounting a real journey. Third-person narrative. Warm, not salesy.

**Clip structure:**
- 0-3s: "She was freezing on every call. 90 days later she was #2 on her team. Here's what changed." — text overlay, no music intro
- 3-25s: Tell the Month 1 → 2 → 3 story in voiceover, overlay stats on each slide
- 25-40s: Walk through the 4-step system she used
- 40-50s: "The tool behind it is Pitchr. Real-time AI coaching."
- 50-55s: CTA — soft, aspirational

**Music vibe:** Uplifting but subtle — a quiet build. The story carries the energy, not the music.

**Alternative angle for TikTok:** Tell the story as a first-person account. "I went from freezing on every call to top 10% in 90 days — here's what I did differently." More personal = more TikTok.
