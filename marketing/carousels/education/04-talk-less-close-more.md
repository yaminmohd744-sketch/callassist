# Carousel 4: Talk Less, Close More

**Platform:** Instagram + TikTok
**Type:** Education
**Target persona:** AE / SDR
**Content angle:** Talk Less, Close More
**Hook goal:** Curiosity + counterintuitive insight (makes them feel like they learned something)

---

## Hook (Slide 1)
**Headline:** Top closers talk less than you do.
**Sub-text:** The talk ratio data that will change how you sell.

**Visual direction:**
- Background: #000000
- Large split-number display: "43%" stacked above "57%" — each in contrasting gradient fills
- "43%" = rep talk time (in muted #814ac8 light tint), "57%" = prospect talk time (in bright #df7afe)
- Caption below: "Top 1% AEs" in small uppercase #f0ebff
- Motion note: Numbers count up independently — 43 and 57 racing to their values simultaneously

## Slide 2
**Headline:** The average sales rep talks 80% of the call.
**Body copy:** They pitch. They fill silence. They over-explain. They mistake talking with selling. Meanwhile, their quota attainment sits below 60%.

**Visual direction:**
- Pie chart (or circular meter): 80% filled in muted red/grey (#ff4444 at 40% opacity) — oversized, clearly "wrong"
- Background #0a0a0a, stat below in #f0ebff

---

## Slide 3
**Headline:** The best closers are professional listeners.
**Body copy:** They ask one question. Then they shut up. The prospect talks themselves into the deal. Silence isn't awkward — it's productive.

**Visual direction:**
- Visual: microphone icon (speaking) vs. headphone icon (listening) — listening icon lit in #39d353, speaking icon greyed
- Clean dark card format with centered icons and body text

---

## Slide 4
**Headline:** Why do reps over-talk? Anxiety.
**Body copy:** When the call feels like it's dying, the instinct is to fill space. But every sentence you add when a prospect is thinking = a mistake. Silence is the close.

**Visual direction:**
- Background: subtle anxiety visual — waveform that starts jagged (over-talking) then flattens (silence)
- Waveform in #814ac8, active region highlighted in #df7afe

---

## Slide 5
**Headline:** The 3 questions every top closer asks.
**Body copy:**
1. "What's the #1 thing stopping you from moving forward today?"
2. "If we could solve [pain they mentioned] — what would that change?"
3. "What would need to be true for this to be a yes?"

Then stop talking.

**Visual direction:**
- Numbered list cards — 3 stacked dark cards (#111111) with #814ac8 numbered labels
- Each question in #f0ebff, clean and readable
- "Then stop talking." below all three in #df7afe italic — punchline delivery

## TikTok text overlay / voiceover notes
**Voiceover tone:** Confident coach energy. Like someone who's watched a hundred reps fail at this. Not condescending — matter-of-fact.

**Clip structure:**
- 0-3s: "If you're talking more than 50% on your sales calls, you're killing your own deals." — instant hook
- 3-12s: Reveal the stat (80% average vs. 43% top 1%). Let the contrast land.
- 12-30s: Explain WHY reps over-talk — anxiety, filling silence
- 30-45s: The 3 questions (rapid voiceover, text on screen)
- 45-55s: Pitchr live talk ratio feature, CTA

**Music vibe:** Focused, slightly jazzy/smooth — "smart people work" energy. Not aggressive.

**Engagement hook:** "What's your talk ratio on your calls? Comment below — I'll tell you what it means."
