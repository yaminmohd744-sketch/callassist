## TikTok text overlay / voiceover notes
**Voiceover tone:** Personal, honest, direct. First-person founder story. No script feel — conversational.

**Clip structure:**
- 0-3s: Face to camera (or text) — "I lost a $40K deal because I froze on the phone. Let me tell you what I built because of it."
- 3-20s: The story — the deal, the question, the blank, the call going cold. Be specific and honest.
- 20-30s: The coaching came Friday. The deal closed Wednesday. Make the timing gap land.
- 30-45s: "What if the coaching had come during the call?" → introduce Pitchr
- 45-55s: Built for individual reps. Not enterprise. Self-serve.
- 55-60s: CTA — "7-day free trial. pitchr.org"

**Music vibe:** Understated, introspective. Maybe a quiet piano or ambient. This is a story — the emotion carries it.

**TikTok format note:** This one works better as a face-to-camera talking-head video with text overlays, not a static carousel. The story is personal — the format should match.
