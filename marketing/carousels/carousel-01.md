# Carousel 1: The Freeze

**Platform:** Instagram + TikTok
**Type:** Pain
**Target persona:** SDR / BDR
**Content angle:** The Freeze Frame
**Hook goal:** Fear + deep validation ("this is exactly me")

---

## Hook (Slide 1)
**Headline:** You know what to say. Until you're on the call.
**Sub-text:** Every SDR knows this feeling.

**Visual direction:**
- Background: #000000 full black with a single subtle vignette glow
- Layout: Full-bleed centered text, headline in two stacked lines
- Colour palette: Headline in #f0ebff near-white, "Until you're on the call." line breaks into #df7afe magenta for contrast
- Typography: Display weight, large — headline ~72-80pt. Stark contrast to black background.
- Motion note: Headline fades in word by word with a 0.3s delay each — creates tension. The magenta line hits last.

## TikTok text overlay / voiceover notes
**Voiceover tone:** Quiet, direct, almost confessional. Not hype — empathy first.

**Clip structure:**
- 0-3s text overlay: "You know what to say. Until you're on the call." — no music, just a slight ambient hum
- 3-8s: voiceover tells the freeze story — "The prospect says 'we already have a solution' and your brain just... stops."
- 8-20s: Stats delivered in voiceover over the stat slides — let the numbers breathe
- 20-40s: Introduce the concept of the in-ear coach. Show or describe the AI suggestion popping up.
- 40-55s: Soft CTA — "It's called Pitchr. Free trial, link in bio."

**Music vibe:** None, or very low ambient tension music. The silence IS the hook.
