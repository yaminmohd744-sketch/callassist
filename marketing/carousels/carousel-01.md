## Slide 4
**Headline:** Stage 2 — Build certainty in you.
**Body copy:** People buy from people they trust. Belfort trained reps to project confidence, tone, and authority within the first 4 seconds of a call. Your pace, your pitch, your pauses — all of it signals competence. Uncertainty is contagious. So is confidence.
**Visual direction:**

## Slide 5
**Headline:** Stage 3 — Build certainty in the company.
**Body copy:** The prospect must trust the organisation behind the product. Social proof, case studies, tenure, guarantees — this is where most reps go silent. Belfort drilled reps to have three "company proof" lines memorised and ready before every dial.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #111111
- Large "03" watermark, third pill segment lit in the stage progress indicator
- Three short bullet points, each preceded by a small #df7afe dot: "Industry leader since ___." / "X clients trust us." / "Backed by ___."
- Body at 16px in #c4b8e0

## Slide 6
**Headline:** Handle objections before they exist.
**Body copy:** The real straight-line move: Belfort never waited for objections. He "loop" closed — addressing uncertainty proactively during the pitch itself. If a prospect was at a 7/10 on price, he'd raise it and close the loop before they ever said "too expensive."
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #000000 with vertical gradient to #0a0a0a
- Bold statement in large type: "7 out of 10 is not a close." — in #df7afe at 36px
- Explanation text below in #c4b8e0 at 15px
- Thin gradient bar (#814ac8 → #df7afe) across the bottom edge

---

## Slide 7
**Headline:** Create urgency without lying.
**Body copy:** Belfort's urgency was always grounded in reality — limited onboarding slots, pricing windows, competitor timing. The key: urgency must be logical, not pressure. When a prospect understands the cost of waiting, they move. Build scarcity into your offer structure, not your tone of voice.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Clock icon outline in #814ac8, top-left corner
- Headline at 34px in #f0ebff, body at 16px in #c4b8e0
- Thin #814ac8 left-border accent on a callout block: "Urgency isn't pressure. It's information."
