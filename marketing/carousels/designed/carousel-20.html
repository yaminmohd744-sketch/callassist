Days 1–30

Shut up and listen.

Your job in the first 30 days is not to perform. It's to understand. Map how the team actually works vs. how it's supposed to work. Those are almost always different.

Shadow every rep who'll let you

Listen to 10 recorded calls minimum

Ask “what do you wish you'd known?” — 3 people

Don't pitch your ideas for improvement yet.

Shadow the top rep, not the best talker.

The loud rep

Confident energy. High volume presence. Easy to mistake for elite.

Watch them if you want.

The quiet closer ✦

Consistently hits quota with the least drama. Has a process. Not winging it. Built something repeatable.

Study this one.

They've built something repeatable. And quiet.

That's what you're learning to replicate.

Days 1–30 — Bonus Task

Build your personal objection library.

Every call — live or recorded — write down every objection. By day 30 you should have 15–20 with at least two response approaches each.

Objection Response approaches
Too expensive Reframe to ROI / Ask what value would justify it
Not right time Ask what would need to change / create urgency
Already have a solution Ask where it falls short / surface the gap

This is your first competitive advantage.

Days 31–60

Run calls. Track everything.

Your job is to generate data, not just dials. Track these four things every single week:

Your talk-to-listen ratio

Where in the call you're losing people

Which objections you're handling vs. dropping

Which openings get you past 60 seconds

The review habit. Every week without fail.

Days 31–60 — Script Timing

When to personalise your script.

Not before you've run 50 calls with the standard one. Scripts aren't cages — but they're important scaffolding while you're learning the structure.

How to not burn out in your first 90 days.

The reps who last are not the ones who work the most hours in month one. They're the ones who set a hard stop — and use the hour before bed for something that has nothing to do with sales.

🏋️
Gym
📖
Reading
🚶
Walking

You cannot process the emotional weight of rejection without a reset routine.

Build it before you need it.
