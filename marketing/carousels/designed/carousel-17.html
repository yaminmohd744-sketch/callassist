Mistake 2

Eye Contact

You're looking at the screen,
not the camera.

👀
Looking at screen
Appears slightly down and off-centre — the universal signal of evasiveness.
📸
Looking at camera

Mistake 4

Speech Rate

Talking too fast reads as anxiety.

Speech rate under stress increases

+25%

Prospects can hear it even when they can't name it.

Slow down 20%. Pause one full second after they finish.

That pause reads as confidence. In every culture. Without exception.

Mistake 5

Nodding

Nodding constantly says you're desperate for approval.

Constant nodding is a submission signal. It says “I agree with everything, please keep liking me.” It undermines your authority and makes every “yes” you give meaningless.

💡

Reserve your nod for genuinely interesting points.

Let silence do the work when they say something you already know.
