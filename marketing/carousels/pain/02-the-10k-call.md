## TikTok text overlay / voiceover notes
**Voiceover tone:** Controlled intensity. Speak like an AE who's been in high-stakes closes. Confident but not bro-y.

**Clip structure:**
- 0-3s: "$10,000. One call. No second chance." — text slam cut on screen, no music
- 3-15s: Walk through the 4-month deal setup in voiceover. Make the stakes feel real.
- 15-30s: The "we need to think about it" moment — let that land before offering the solution
- 30-45s: Introduce Pitchr's close probability + buying signal detection. Show the UI.
- 45-55s: Follow-up email angle, then CTA.

**Music vibe:** Low-key tension/pressure music — something minimal and cinematic. Not celebratory yet.
