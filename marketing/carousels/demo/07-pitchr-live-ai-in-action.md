## TikTok text overlay / voiceover notes
**Voiceover tone:** Demo energy — excited but controlled. "Let me show you something you haven't seen before."

**Clip structure:**
- 0-3s: Screen recording or UI demo clip — show the objection card popping up on screen. No intro — just show the product first.
- 3-10s: "This is Pitchr. It listens to your call and tells you what to say. In real time."
- 10-25s: Walk through objection detection → suggestion → buying signal → close probability rising
- 25-40s: Post-call summary + email generation
- 40-50s: "Works on any call — phone, Zoom, whatever you use."
- 50-55s: CTA

**Music vibe:** Slightly futuristic/techy ambient — conveys "this is new and different." Not hype, more awe.

**Pro tip:** If possible, use actual screen recording of the Pitchr UI on these slides — even a clean mockup feels more credible than a static graphic for a demo carousel.
