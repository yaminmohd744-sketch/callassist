## Slide 6
**Headline:** How commission structures actually work.
**Body copy:** Most plans: tiered accelerators. Hit 100% of quota = 10% commission. Hit 120% = 15% on everything above 100%. Hit 80% = 7% (decelerator). The structure is designed so the top 20% of reps earn disproportionately more. That's the game. Play it at the top.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Headline: #df7afe ExtraBold, 36px
- Tiered commission visual — three horizontal bars:
  - <100%: smaller, #814ac8 faint
  - 100%: medium, #814ac8 solid
  - 120%+: large, gradient fill (#814ac8 → #df7afe)
- "Play it at the top." — isolated, #f0ebff, bold — the motivational payoff

## Slide 7
**Headline:** The questions to ask about comp before you sign.
**Body copy:** 1. What % of reps hit OTE last year? 2. What did the median rep earn in total cash? 3. Are accelerators uncapped above 150%? 4. Does quota reset quarterly or annually? 5. Is there a draw, and is it recoverable? These five questions change the negotiation entirely.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #111111
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 34px
- Numbered list — large #814ac8 numbers, white text per question
- Each question on its own line, clear vertical spacing
- Screenshot-optimised layout — this is a save-and-use slide
