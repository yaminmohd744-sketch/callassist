## Slide 7
**Headline:** How to rewire your relationship with rejection.
**Body copy:** 1. Before each call, say: "This might be a no. That's fine." 2. After a rejection, name one thing you did well. 3. Track your ratio — when no's feel random, data makes them feel logical. 4. Debrief fast, move on faster.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #111111
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 36px
- Numbered list formatted with large #814ac8 numbers, white text beside each
- Each item on its own line with clear vertical spacing
