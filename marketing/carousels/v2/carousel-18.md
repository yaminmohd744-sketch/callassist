## Slide 6
**Headline:** Layer 5: Call intelligence — your post-call coach.
**Body copy:** Gong. Chorus. Wingman. These tools record, transcribe, and analyse calls after the fact. They surface talk ratios, filler word frequency, objection patterns, and which phrases correlate with wins vs losses. They're the closest thing to a coach sitting on every call — except they never sleep and never have a bad day.
**Visual direction:**

## Slide 7
**Headline:** Layer 6: Real-time coaching — the missing piece.
**Body copy:** Call recording tells you what went wrong after the call ends. Real-time coaching tells you what to do while the call is still happening. When an objection surfaces, a buying signal appears, or a silence runs too long — the coaching fires in the moment when it can still change the outcome. Most stacks stop at Layer 5. This is the gap.
