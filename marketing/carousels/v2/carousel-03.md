# Carousel 03: What Nobody Tells You About Sales

**Category:** Honest
**Platform:** Instagram + TikTok
**Target persona:** New reps, people considering sales as a career, anyone 1–3 years in who feels the gap between expectation and reality
**Hook goal:** Validation / Empathy

---

## Hook (Slide 1)
**Headline:** They told me sales was easy money.
**Sub-text:** Nobody mentioned this part.

**Visual direction:**
- Background: #000000
- Headline: #f0ebff Figtree ExtraBold, 54px, centred
- Sub-text: #df7afe, Regular italic, 22px, below with spacing
- Visual element: No icons. Pure typography play. The sub-text should feel like it's leaning in — slightly offset right or in a looser tracking weight
- Motion note: Headline appears first. Sub-text fades in after 0.5s delay — the reveal lands harder.

---

## Slide 2
**Headline:** Nobody told you rejection has a physical feeling.
**Body copy:** The moment before you dial a number you don't want to call — that tension in your chest is real. It doesn't mean you're weak. It means you're human. The best reps feel it too. They've just stopped letting it make the decision.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 36px
- Body copy: white, Regular, 17px, generous line height (1.7)
- Layout: full-width text block — this is an emotional slide, no distracting icons
- A very subtle heartbeat line graphic behind the text in #814ac8 at 6% opacity — EKG style

---

## Slide 3
**Headline:** It takes longer to get good than anyone admits.
**Body copy:** "You'll ramp in 60 days." Sure. But real fluency — where you can handle any objection, read any prospect, and control a call — that takes 18 months minimum. Nobody says this because it doesn't help with hiring.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #111111
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 36px
- "18 months minimum" bolded in #df7afe, slightly larger than surrounding text
- Body copy: white regular
- Right side: a subtle timeline bar graphic — short "hired" label at left, long empty bar, "actually good" label far right with a small marker — minimalist illustration in #814ac8

---

## Slide 4
**Headline:** Most sales training doesn't work on real calls.
**Body copy:** The roleplay is perfect. Your script is memorised. Then someone asks "why should I switch from what I'm using now?" and your brain empties. Training builds knowledge. Repetition on live calls builds skill. They're not the same thing.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Headline: #df7afe ExtraBold, 36px
- "Your brain empties." — this phrase isolated on its own line, #f0ebff, largest text in the body block
- Rest of body copy: white, regular
- Visual: two small icons side by side — a textbook (training) and a phone (skill) — with "≠" between them in #814ac8

---

## Slide 5
**Headline:** Activity and results are not the same thing.
**Body copy:**100 calls a day sounds impressive. But if you're calling the wrong people, leading with the wrong message, and never reviewing what's working — you're just building scar tissue, not skill. Volume without feedback is just practice at being average.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #111111
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 36px
- "Practice at being average." — isolated, italic, #df7afe, the emotional punch of the slide
- Body copy: white regular
- Background graphic: a faint tally mark count (IIII with the fifth crossing) in very light #814ac8 — representing volume without meaning

---

## Slide 6
**Headline:** The emotional toll is real and nobody budgets for it.
**Body copy:** Getting told no 40 times before lunch affects you. Even when you rationally know it's not personal. The reps who last aren't the ones without feelings — they're the ones with a routine for managing them. Gym. Walk. Call debrief. Anything that creates a reset.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 36px
- Body copy: white regular, generous spacing
- The three items ("Gym. Walk. Call debrief.") shown as a small horizontal list in #df7afe with a dot separator between each
- No icons needed. This slide earns shares through honesty alone.

---

## Slide 7
**Headline:** The rep next to you who "makes it look easy" has a system.
**Body copy:** They're not calmer. They're not more naturally charming. They've mapped their objections. They've built their own playbook. They know the five most likely paths a call will take and they've prepared for each one. That's not talent. That's preparation.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #111111
- Headline: #f0ebff ExtraBold, 36px
- "That's not talent. That's preparation." — isolated, larger, #df7afe
- Faint network/map lines in background at 5% opacity — representing preparation paths
- Body copy: white regular

---

## Slide 8
**Headline:** None of this is to scare you.
**Body copy:** Sales is still one of the fastest ways to build an income, a network, and a set of transferable skills that work everywhere. But knowing the real version of it — not the pitch deck version — is what separates reps who last from reps who burn out at 9 months.
**Visual direction:**
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Tone shift: warmer energy. Headline in #df7afe, body in white
- Layout: centred, balanced. Bit more whitespace than previous slides.
- This is the bridge to the CTA — reassuring and empowering

## TikTok Adaptation Notes

- Voiceover tone: confessional, honest, not self-pitying — like talking to a friend over coffee
- Text overlays: each headline appears as the creator speaks, reinforcing the point
- Music vibe: stripped back, lo-fi acoustic — something reflective and warm
- Pacing: slower than other carousels — this content earns time with emotional resonance
- Hook line for video: "I was terrible at sales for the first 18 months. Here's what nobody told me." — say this on camera in the first 3 seconds.
