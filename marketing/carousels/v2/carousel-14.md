## Slide 4
**Headline:** What the research actually says about rejection.
**Body copy:** The brain processes social rejection in the same neural pathway as physical pain. A cold call hang-up activates the same region as being physically hurt. You're not being dramatic — your brain is doing exactly what it evolved to do. The problem is it evolved for a world where social rejection had much higher stakes than a dial going to voicemail.

## Slide 6
**Headline:** The physical reset (it works).
**Body copy:** Before a difficult call: take 3 slow breaths, lower your shoulders deliberately, relax your jaw. These aren't spiritual practices — they activate your parasympathetic nervous system and reduce cortisol within 60 seconds. You will feel physically calmer. Calm sounds different on the phone. Prospects can hear it.

## Slide 7
**Headline:** How to rewire your relationship with the dial.
**Body copy:** Don't set outcome goals for the first 20 calls of a session. Set process goals: "My goal is to open well and ask one good question." Remove the result from the equation. When your brain learns that a dial doesn't require a yes to be a success, the anticipation anxiety drops dramatically. You're retraining the threat model.

- Body copy: white Regular
