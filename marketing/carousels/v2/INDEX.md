## The 5 to Post First — for Fastest Audience Growth

### 1. Carousel 01 — Things Every SDR Has Said At Least Once
**Why first:** Highest immediate recognition rate. Every sales rep has said all seven of these phrases. It earns the follow because it validates the audience's experience before asking them for anything. Comments will flood with "I literally said this today." High share rate because tagging a colleague is the natural response. This is your audience-building post.

### 2. Carousel 10 — Salary Transparency
**Why second:** Salary content reliably goes viral on Instagram and TikTok. The "no recruiter spin" framing makes it the trustworthy version of content everyone already wants. It attracts a wide audience beyond active sales reps — anyone considering sales as a career, anyone evaluating an offer, anyone curious about how commissions work. High save rate. High follow rate. Maximum reach.

### 3. Carousel 16 — Sales vs Normal Jobs
**Why third:** Humour carousels get shared outside the core audience. A sales rep shares this with their non-sales partner, sibling, or friend who "doesn't get it." That share introduces the account to a new audience who may not follow sales content otherwise. The end-of-quarter slide and the payday slide are genuinely funny to people who have no sales experience. This is your cross-audience post.

### 4. Carousel 14 — Cold Calling Anxiety Is Real
**Why fourth:** Emotional vulnerability creates the deepest engagement. Comments on this post will be long, personal, and from people who feel genuinely seen. That comment volume signals to the algorithm that the content is resonant — and the replies allow you to build real relationships with followers. This post earns trust. It's the one that converts a casual follower into someone who actively recommends the account.

### 5. Carousel 07 — The 30-Second Rule
**Why fifth:** Pure tactical value. It gives the audience something they can use on their very next call. High save rate. Reps screenshot the template slide. This post establishes the account as a practical coaching resource, not just an entertainment account. After posts 1–4 have built audience breadth and trust, post 7 shows that the account delivers actionable value — which is what converts followers into pitchr.org visitors.

## Notes for the Posting Team

**Captions:** Every carousel has a pre-written Instagram caption in its file. Use them as starting points — adapt to match what's happening in the sales community that week if relevant.

**Hashtags:** Each file includes 5 hashtags. Keep them. Add 2–3 trending/niche hashtags from the week if applicable.

**TikTok:** Every carousel file ends with TikTok Adaptation Notes. These are not afterthoughts — adapt the carousel structure and voiceover before posting there. The visual format, pacing, and hook are different enough to matter.

**Pitchr.org CTA:** Every carousel ends at pitchr.org with a free trial offer. Do not change or omit this. The CTA slide design is consistent across all 20 carousels — full gradient background, large white headline, "Start free" pill button.
