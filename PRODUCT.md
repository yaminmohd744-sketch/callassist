# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Sales reps and founders who run high-volume outbound calls and video meetings. They work fast, often alone, and want to close deals without drowning in admin. Some use the live AI co-pilot during calls; others deploy an autonomous AI avatar to handle meetings entirely while they watch from the sideline or do something else.

## Product Purpose

Pitchbase is an all-in-one AI sales platform. It handles the full sales workflow: live call coaching, autonomous AI avatars for video meetings, lead management, analytics, and post-call reporting. The goal is to let a single rep perform like a team.

## Brand Personality

Precise, powerful, unfussy. Like a Bloomberg terminal that actually wants to be used.

## Anti-references

- Salesforce: bloated, corporate, soul-destroying
- Generic SaaS dashboards with pastel cards and rounded everything
- AI tools that look like chatbots slapped with a dashboard shell

## Design Principles

1. **Data at the surface.** The most important information is always visible without a click. No buried stats.
2. **Density without clutter.** Pack meaning in, not elements. Whitespace is earned, not defaulted.
3. **Status always known.** Live, scheduled, done — the user should never wonder what state a thing is in.
4. **Actions are obvious.** One clear primary action per screen. Everything else is secondary.
5. **Dark by nature.** This tool runs during calls and meetings, often in dim environments. Dark is functional, not aesthetic.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion supported via prefers-reduced-motion. Color is never the only signal for status.
